    Description:

    Since the middle ages and before, human societies have suffered from the problem of excess waste, it happens that sometimes people are forced to throw something, unwillingly, just because it will expire soon, or maybe they think that it has no use. In our days this problem still exists every day, which is people sometimes throw things, sometimes reluctantly, that others might find useful. Here is where our application comes to play!.

    Our application is an interactive platform that has multiple functionalities, the majority of them are user-based, all oriented towards fixing this problem. Our application offers solutions for both classes of people, the people who are reluctant to discard something that other people might find useful, and the people who could use it and benefit from might and might even save them money or grant them a profit.
